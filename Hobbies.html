Dance

I have been dancing for 16 years. I mainly study ballet and modern, but I have done various other styles as well. For the last 5 years I was a student at Ballet West Academy. 3 of those years I was in the professional training program.

I also was part of the contemporary performing group at Ballet West Academy for my last year. I had the opportunity to dance on the Eccles Theater Stage and perform for a charity fundraiser for Primary Children's Hospital.

Being with friends

It is important for me to surround myself with good friends. I become better when I find people that are good to me and help me to become the best version of myself. As I get to know other people I can better understand myself.

Photography

I love to take pictures and mess around with different cameras. It is a great way to express myself and see the world in a different way.

Go on Trips

I love to travel to different places. Lately I have loved going on small trips with friends and spending time away from school. It is a great way to be spontaneous and live in the moment.
