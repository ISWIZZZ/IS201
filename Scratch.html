Charlotte White- Favorite Hobby





Ballet

My all time favorite hobby is dance. I have been doing it since I was 3 and it has made me who I am today. I mainly do ballet, but I have a great passion for modern and contemporary dance. At the age of 13 I went to Ballet West Academy to reecive better training. I am so happy I chose to go to Ballet West because I was able to meet some incredible people, learn from amazing faculty, and grow as an artist.

After being at Ballet West for 2 years, I joined the professional training program. This would mean I would have to give up time at school and put in more hours at dance. With this sacrifice I was blessed in so many ways. It ended up helping my school work, expanding my friendship circle, and allowing me time with my family in the evenings.

These are some experiences I had from dancing with the professional training program:

  1. Classes with guest teachers
  2. Higher roles in shows
  3. More rigorous training
  4. Movement Mentor
    • helping kids with disabilities learn to dance
    • Become a mentor to aspiring dancers
